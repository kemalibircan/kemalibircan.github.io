Otostop

The development phase is still ongoing. It is a hitchhiking app. You specify your destination and if there are those who want to hitchhike on this road, they get into your car for a certain fee through the application. In this way, you reduce your travel expenses.

I used many technologies while developing the application. The main technologies I used: React-Native, Node.js, Express.js, PostgreSQL. Inside the application, I used Redux for state management, Google Directions, Google Maps, api for route information, distance calculation, location information. I used TCP protocol for messaging. In addition, I developed the application with the support of many libraries.
